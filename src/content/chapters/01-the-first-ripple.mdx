## The Science Behind Ripples

Did you know that scientists have proven that our actions really do create ripples? It's not just pretend!

### Mirror Neurons

Special brain cells called "mirror neurons" make people copy what they see! When you smile, their brain fires in just milliseconds, automatically making them want to smile back!

### The 3-Person Rule

Scientists found that your actions can influence up to 3 degrees of separation -- that means your friend's friend's friend!

### The Butterfly Effect

Some scientists think even tiny actions (like a butterfly flapping its wings) can cause big changes far away! This is a cool theory from the study of complex systems.
